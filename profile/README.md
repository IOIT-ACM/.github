The IOIT ACM Student Chapter is a student community at AISSMS IOIT. We don't just attend tech, we build it. From internal tooling and event platforms to community service initiatives, we put what we learn into practice.
 
## Get Involved
 
We're always working on something. Here's how to find your way in:
 
- [Explore our events and initiatives](https://ioit.acm.org/)
- [Browse our open source repositories](https://github.com/orgs/IOIT-ACM/repositories)
- [Join the chapter or collaborate with us](mailto:acm.studentbranch@aissmsioit.org)
- For questions about a specific project, open an issue in that repository rather than reaching out by email.
---
 
We uphold the [ACM Code of Ethics](https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics). Visit [ioit.acm.org](https://ioit.acm.org) to learn more.
