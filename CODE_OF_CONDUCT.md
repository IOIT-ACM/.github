# Code of Conduct

All projects and spaces under the IOIT ACM Student Chapter are governed by the [ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct](https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics).

We expect everyone interacting with our repositories - contributors, maintainers, and community members to uphold these principles: honesty, respect, fairness, and a commitment to the public good.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these principles, please reach out to us at [acm.studentbranch@aissmsioit.org](mailto:acm.studentbranch@aissmsioit.org). All reports are handled with discretion.
